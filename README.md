## Demos

*Official Babylon.js demos that really showcase what it is capable of.*

* [ActionBuilder](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/actionbuilder/) - Turn the lights on/off using flip switches. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/ActionBuilder)
* [Actions](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/actions/) - A torus that goes around in a circle and changes shape when it hovers over another shape. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Actions)
* [Amp360Video](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/amp360video/) - Explore a video in 360Â°. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Amp360Video)
* [AssetsManager](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/assetsmanager/) - A human skull. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/AssetsManager)
* [AudioAnalyser](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/audioanalyser/) - Babylon isn't just graphics; it's a full game engine. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/AudioAnalyser)
* [Bones](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/bones/) - A walking soldier + animated bunnies. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Bones)
* [Boom](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/boom/) - Make things explode with a touch. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Boom)
* [Bump](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/bump/) - Render a ball using bump mapping. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Bump)
* [CellShading](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/cellshading/) - Use cell-shading to reproduce a cartoon-like effect. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/CellShading)
* [Charting](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/charting/) - 3D charts. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Charting)
* [ChibiRex](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/chibirex/) - An animated baby T-Rex. What more do you need? (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/ChibiRex)
* [Dancers](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/dancers/) - Animated 3D dancers. *warning: takes a long time to load.* (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Dancers)
* [Dancing CSG](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/dancing%20csg/) - Constructive Solid Geometry using lights and a sparkly texture. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Dancing%20CSG)
* [Decals](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/decals/) - Touch the cat to splatter it with black ink. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Decals)
* [DiStraction](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/distraction/) - A Javascript driving game demo. Works with a DS3. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Distraction)
* [DragNDrop](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/dragndrop/) - Drag and rearrange different shapes. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/DragNDrop)
* [Espilit](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/espilit/) - Explore an entire (barebones) house. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Espilit)
* [ExtrudePolygon](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/extrudepolygon/) - Destroy your own house. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/ExtrudePolygon)
* [Facets](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/facets/) - A fountain of countless balls falling onto a complex shape, showcasing physics. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Facets)
* [Flat 2009](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/flat2009/) - Explore an extremely detailed apartment, including a fishtank and bathroom.(https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Flat2009)
* [FlightHelmet](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/flighthelmet/) - A realistic aviator's helmet. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/FlightHelmet)
* [Fur](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/fur/) - A disturbingly furred bunny. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Fur)
* [GLTF](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/gltf/) - A GLTF Alien model featuring facial animations. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/GLTF)
* [GUI](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/gui/) - An interactive 3D UI. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/GUI)
* [GlowLayer](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/glowlayer/) - Make a neon pipe glow like plasma. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/GlowLayer)
* [GlowingEspilit](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/glowingespilit/) - Espilit but with glowing effects. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/GlowingEspilit)
* [Heightmap](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/heightmap/) - Generate terrain using a heightmap. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Heightmap)
* [HillValley](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/hillvalley/) - Explore a town isolated from the outside world, like in *The Twilight Zone.* (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/HillValley)
* [HillValleyVR](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/hillvalleyvr/) - HillValley but creepier in VR. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/HillValleyVR)
* [InstancedBones](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/instancedbones/) - A group of soldiers marching in sync. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/InstancedBones)
* [Instances2](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/instances2/) - A complex, moving space engine. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Instances2)
* [LookAt](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/lookat/) - Stare at a colored ball moving around in a box of shapes. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/LookAt)
* [Mansion](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/mansion/) - Explore a haunted mansion. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Mansion)
* [MansionVR](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/mansionvr/) - Explore a haunted mansion in virtual reality. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/MansionVR)
* [MotionBlur](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/motionblur/) - Blur things like if you were moving like The Flash. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/MotionBlur)
* [PBRGlossy](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/pbrglossy/) - Physically based rendering of a battle damaged sci-fi helmet. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/PBRGlossy)
* [PBRGlossyBloom](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/pbrglossybloom/) - Similar to PBRGlossy, but with an extra bloom filter. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/PBRGlossyBloom)
* [PPBloom](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/ppbloom/) - Simple post-processing. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/PPBloom)
* [PPConvolution](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/ppconvolution/) - A more advanced post-processing demo. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/PPConvolution)
* [Particles](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/particles/) - Colorful particles erupting. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Particles)
* [Physics](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/physics/) - Spheres and blocks with gravity added. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Physics)
* [Polygon](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/polygon/) - It's a bird, but how did it get so flat? (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Polygon)
* [Procedural](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/procedural/) - Don't hard-code content; generate it procedurally. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Procedural)
* [Retail](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/retail/) - Explore a small store (no stealing). (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Retail)
* [SPS](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/sps/) - Simulate asteroids around a planet using a solid particle system. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/SPS)
* [SPSCollisions](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/spscollisions/) - Solid particles as confetti being raining down on a ball. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/SPSCollisions)
* [SpaceDeK](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/spacedek/) - Pretty darn close to a space-shooter. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/SpaceDeK)
* [Sponza](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/sponza/) - The most famous Babylon.js demo. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Sponza)
* [SponzaDynamicShadows](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/sponzadynamicshadows/) - A simpler version of Sponza, but with dynamic lights. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/SponzaDynamicShadows)
* [TheCar](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/thecar/) - A futuristic car parked all by itself in a garage. Too bad there's no key. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/TheCar)
* [Train](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/train/) - An incredibly realistic train. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Train)
* [Tunnel](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/tunnel/) - Travel inside an endless rust-filled tunnel. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/Tunnel)
* [V8](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/v8/) - A complex, moving replica of a V8 engine. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/V8)
* [WCafe](https://www.babylonjs.com/demos/wcafe/) - Windows Cafe is a great place to unwind and have a relaxing look around. (https://github.com/BabylonJS/CDN/tree/master/Demos/WCafe)
